Proverbs Daily

Christian scripture daily reading uses an API to make an easy quiz for the Proverb of the day.



This project solves a problem in the Christian community for people who want add the discipline of daily scripture reading into their lives. The quiz like structure takes the reader verse by verse through the chapter of the day to add a level of mental interaction just above reading. If you can take this project to the next level, I am open to proposals, as this solution is a major need in the Christian market.
